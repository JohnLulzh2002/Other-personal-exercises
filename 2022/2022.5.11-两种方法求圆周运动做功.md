$dW=M\cdot d\theta$
$$
\begin{aligned}
M&=\frac l2\cos\theta\cdot mg\\
W&=\int_0^\frac\pi2M\cdot d\theta\\
&=\int_0^\frac\pi2\frac l2\cos\theta\cdot mg\cdot d\theta\\
&=\frac l2mg\int_0^\frac\pi2\cos\theta\cdot d\theta&\cdots(*)\\
&=\frac l2mg\int_0^1d\sin\theta\\
&=\frac l2mg\\
\end{aligned}
$$
